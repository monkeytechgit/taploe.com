Your questions, answered

Everything you need to know about Taploe.

How does the Taploe card work?

Tap your card on an NFC-enabled phone or scan the QR code. The digital profile opens instantly so people can save your contact, message you or access your links and social profiles.

Is it compatible with iPhone and Android?

Yes. Taploe works with NFC-enabled phones and through the QR code printed on the card.

Can I update my information?

Yes. You can update your details, social profiles, links and content without replacing the physical card.

Does Taploe work for large teams?

Yes. The teams solution lets you manage profiles, cards and brand identity from one place.

Is my information secure?

Your profile only shows the information you choose to publish, and you can update or remove it whenever you need to.

Does the card need a battery or charging?

No. The Taploe card does not need a battery or charging. NFC works without onboard power and is ready anytime.

What information can I share?

Phone, email, WhatsApp, social profiles, website, calendar, location, documents and other links.

Does it include the digital profile?

Yes. NFC cards include a digital profile linked to the chip and QR code.

Does it work anywhere in the world?

Yes. The profile can be opened anywhere with internet access.

How long does shipping take?

Estimated production and shipping time is confirmed at checkout based on quantity and destination.
