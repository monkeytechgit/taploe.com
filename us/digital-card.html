FAQ

The essentials to start with your digital card.

Is the digital card free?

Yes. You can create a free digital card to start, share it by link or QR and update your information whenever you need to.

Do I need to buy an NFC card?

No. The NFC card is an optional add-on. Your digital card works without a physical card.

Does the other person need to install an app?

No. Anyone who receives your digital card can open it in the browser and save your details without installing apps.

What can I share?

Contact details, WhatsApp, email, social profiles, website, portfolio, calendar, catalog and other important links.

Can I edit it later?

Yes. You can update your details, links and personal brand from your account without changing the QR or link.

Which features are Pro?

Features like leads, analytics and advanced tracking may require Taploe Pro.
