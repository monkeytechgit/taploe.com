FAQ for companies.

Answers to common questions about Taploe for teams and organizations.

Is there a minimum card quantity?

Yes. NFC card projects for teams start at 5 cards, either NFC cards, metal NFC cards or a combination of both.

Can each team member have different information?

Yes. Each profile is individual and can include a different name, role, contact details, links, social profiles and calendar.

Can we use our company colors and logo?

Yes. We apply your company visual identity to profiles and card designs.

What happens when an employee changes roles?

Their information is updated from the platform and reflected on the same profile without replacing the card.

Can I deactivate a team member’s profile?

Yes. An admin can deactivate it temporarily or permanently when needed.

Are cards delivered configured?

Yes. Each card is linked to its matching profile and delivered ready to use.

Can we add new team members later?

Yes. You can request additional profiles and cards as your team grows or changes.

Who can manage profiles?

The admins your company defines. Permissions can be organized according to each user’s responsibilities.

How long does production take?

Average production time is 3 to 5 business days after approving designs and confirming information.

Do you ship to different branches?

Yes. We can organize shipping to a main address or different branches depending on project needs.
